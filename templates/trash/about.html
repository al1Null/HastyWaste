  • How it Works

    Hasty Waste tracks your garbage for the purpose of efficiency, control, and cost reduction. Hasty Waste uses state of the art ultrasonic and GPS technology to allow customers to access each Hasty Waste bin in a building, create and pull maintanence requests, determine the best time to take out each bin, and see how full each bin is.

    What it Offers

    Each Hasty Waste bin connects with other bins to determine the best time to take out all bins in the local area using past take-out information. This over time greatly diminishes the amount of time and waste that a company uses by ensuring every bin is taken out at every point in time.

    Prioritizing Safety

    Each Hasty Waste bin is also equipped with temperature and liquid sensors in order to report safety concerns such as a leaked bag, or a fire within a bin. This allows each customer to have a safe and enjoyable experience with their Hasty Waste bins.

    Complete Control

    Each Hasty Waste bin can connect with the Hasty Base to get all analytic information in an administrator tab. At the click of a mouse, all Hasty Waste bin details and locations can be viewed.

    Data Optimization

    We analyse the past data of each bin to generate a more accurate prediction of when a bin will be full.
